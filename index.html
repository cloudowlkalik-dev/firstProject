Night Fury

Appearence

  • Black scales
  • Green eyes
  • Large wings
  • Fins right behind wings and at end of tail

Diet

  • Fish
  • Will eat other meats but prefers fish
  • Does not like eel

Abilities

  • Plasma blasts
  • Echolocation
  • Retractable teeth
  • Spikes can split to help with tight turns while flying
  • If spikes are split they can turn blue giving the dragon more powerful plasma blasts
  • While blue a night fury can channel lighting and turn invisible

Other

  • Sometimes called The Unholy Offspring of Lightning and Death Itself
  • There is only one night fury left: Toothless
  • The night furies were hunted by Grimmel the Grizzly
  • Toothless is the alpha of all the dragons
